About Kidney Diseases
:
Kidneys filter wastes and excess fluids from your blood. Kidney diseases can cause a buildup of fluid or body waste or electrolyte problems.

Loss of kidney function can cause:
  • High blood pressure
  • Urinating more or less
  • Nausea or vomiting
  • Shortness of breath or chest pain
  • Decreased mental sharpness
